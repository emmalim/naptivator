FEATURES


LOCATION-BASED ALARM

The Naptivator alerts users when they are within range of their destination through gentle vibrations.

PERSONAL

The vibration motors placed directly on the wearer's temples make the alarm system personal, reducing disruptiveness to other bus passengers.



DISTRACTION-FREE

Our tangible user interface reduces time spent on electronics which minimizes the likelihood of getting distracted.

PROMOTES PRODUCTIVITY

Better nap quality boosts energy and increases productivity throughout the day.
